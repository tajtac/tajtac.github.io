I'm currently a PhD student, supervised by Dr. Adrian Buganza Tepole and Dr. Ilias Bilionis, in the department of Mechanical Engineering, at Purdue University.

I am interested in applying machine learning to problems in mechanics of soft tissue. In my PhD, I focus on the development of data-driven constitutive material models for the hyperelastic and viscoelastic behavior of skin.
